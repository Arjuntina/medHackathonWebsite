What is Nutrishare?

  NutriShare is a groundbreaking nonprofit organization and mobile app dedicated to delivering nutritious food for free to underserved individuals and families in the Boston area. What sets NutriShare apart is its innovative approach to food distribution and community engagement.
  At the core of NutriShare's mission is its unique collaboration with college community gardens and student labor. Rather than relying solely on traditional food sources, NutriShare partners with local colleges and universities to cultivate their gardens, producing a sustainable source of fresh, organic produce. College students play a pivotal role, actively participating in planting, tending, and harvesting the crops. This not only addresses food scarcity but also fosters community involvement, teaching valuable life skills and connecting students with the larger community.
  The NutriShare app serves as a central hub for users to request deliveries of these fresh, locally-grown, and nutrition-packed foods right to their doorsteps. This model ensures that wholesome, high-quality produce reaches those who need it most while reducing the carbon footprint associated with long-distance food transportation.
  NutriShare is committed to alleviating food insecurity, promoting community engagement, and supporting sustainable practices. This innovative blend of education, community, and technology is driving positive change in the Boston area, setting a model for future nonprofits to tackle food inequality in creative, efficient, and sustainable ways.

Meet Maria

  Maria, a Haitian woman, found her life transformed by Nutrishare, a program designed to address the challenges of individuals who struggle to access nutritious foods due to time constraints. As a busy professional, Maria often faced the daunting task of juggling work and family responsibilities, leaving her with limited time to search for and prepare nutritious meals. Nutrishare came to her rescue, providing her with convenient, ready-to-eat, and nutritionally balanced options that not only saved her time but also improved her overall health. Thanks to Nutrishare, Maria now enjoys a healthier lifestyle, balancing her hectic schedule while still prioritizing her nutrition and well-being.
